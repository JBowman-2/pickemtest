KingBellys Pick'em

Kingbellys Pick'em is a great way to stay excited about football, without having to put in the time that traditional Fanatasy Football requires. Basically you just pick the teams that you think are going to win and cross your fingers.

Weekly Play

Another advantange is it doesn't take a full season commitment. Each week is like starting over and has a winner, so if you miss a week its not as big of a deal. Weekly play cost $2 and its winner take all, so the more people who play the bigger the payout.

Season Jackpot

As an added bonus there is a season jackpot option. The Season jackpot will go to whoever has the most correct picks for the entire regular NFL season. This score will be the total number of correct picks for your best 14 weeks of the season so if you have a few bad weeks or forget make your picks you are still in the running for the Jackpot. The season jackpot is an additional 1 time payment of $10 and is also winner take all, so the more people who play the bigger the jackpot. To be included in the season jackpot the entrance payment needs to be paid by the start of the 4th week - September 26th.

Play Instructions

  1. Select the winner for each game.
  2. Choose a tie breaker score for the final Monday night game.
  3. Make sure and put your name in and it the send picks button.

Rules

  • Picks need to be submitted by the start of the Thursday night game for the week to get points for all games. Picks can be submitted after the Thursday night game but it will result in a 0 for the Thursday night game pick. Picks final deadline is before the start of any game on Sunday, any picks sent after this wont be valid.
  • Only 1 submission of picks will count per play each week.
  • You can submit your picks multiple times, but the picks closest to the cut off time (thursday night game time) will be the picks that are kept and the previous submissions will be voided.
  • Tie breakers will be decided by the score of the final Monday night game. Whoever is closest to the scores wins.
  • If there is still a tie after using the tie breaker rule, the winner will be whoever submitted the picks being used first.
  • Season Jackpot tie breakers will be decided by whoever has the highest single weekly score (if both of their highest single weekly scores are the same it goes to the next highest until one of the players has a higher weekly score than the other).
